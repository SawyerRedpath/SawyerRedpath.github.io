About Me


My name is Sawyer Redpath and I am a Software Developer from Calgary, Alberta, Canada currently living in Cleveland, Ohio, USA. I am currently going to school at Cuyahoga Community College pursuing an Associate Degree in Programming & Development. I recently graduated from Tech Elevator: a coding bootcamp based in Cleveland, where I received my .NET developer certificate. Prior to living in Cleveland I went to the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology where I took several classes towards a Software Deveplopment Diploma. When I'm not in class I am working from home on a variety of side projects and online courses/videos to learn more and improve as a developer.

I am always looking to be more involved in the local tech community so please if you are interested in collaborating on a side project together, or looking for a junior level full stack developer to mentor, or are new to programming and would like some mentorship from myself, and anything in between please reach out to me.

Users can review rooms after completing them and score rooms, visitors to the website are able to get a list of escape rooms in a given radius of their location (eg. 5km, 10km, 25km, etc.), and more! Developed with ASP.NET Core MVC.

National Parks Weather Service (Completed at Tech Elevator)

A web application developed with the ASP.NET Core MVC framework. Users can view National Parks and detail info for each park, view a 5 day weather forecast for any park and convert from Fahrenheit to Celsius (their selection is stored in session and remembered). Users can fill out a survey about their favorite national park which is then sent to a database.
